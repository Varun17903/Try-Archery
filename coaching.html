Coaching at Bendigo Target Archery Club

Our club offers coaching sessions for archers of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced competitors. Whether you're just starting out or looking to improve your technique, our experienced coaches are here to help you reach your goals.

Levels of Coaches

  • Level 1, Archery Instructors: Approximately 20 active Archery Instructors.
  • Level 2, Coaches: Jenny, Melissa, David, and Adrian.
  • Level 3, Club and Regional Coach: Shelley.

Assistance and Workshops

Basic coaching help is available to all archers on most Saturday mornings from 9:00 to 11:30. Level 2 and 3 Coaches offer assistance with measuring and advising on your first bow purchase and setup. More experienced archers may make arrangements for coaching by seeing the Club Coach.

Coaches and instructors deserve some time themselves to shoot, so please respect their time in the afternoon.

General workshop/seminar sessions are held each month for all members, covering topics like 'Bow Set Up and Tuning', 'Purchasing the correct Arrows', 'Arrow Fletching and Maintenance', etc.

Coaching Tips

Hot Weather Precautions

  • Wear a hat and sunscreen.
  • Watch for signs of dehydration and drink plenty of water.
  • Retreat to the shade between ends.

Remember, it's sometimes better to sit out in the shade and spectate in very hot weather.

Risks during Storms and Strong Winds

  • Never shoot outside as a storm approaches.
  • Do not shelter under trees in windy weather.

Purchasing your First Bow

New members are encouraged not to buy a bow until consulting with a coach. Hire equipment initially and then get measured and discuss options with a coach.
