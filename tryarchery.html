Try Archery at Bendigo Target Archery Club

Interested in trying out archery? Our club offers introductory sessions for beginners to experience the thrill of shooting a bow and arrow in a safe and supportive environment.

Beginner Sessions

Our beginner sessions are designed for individuals who have little to no experience with archery. During these sessions, you'll receive basic instruction on archery techniques, safety guidelines, and equipment usage.

Our experienced coaches will guide you through the fundamentals of archery, including proper stance, posture, and aiming. All necessary equipment will be provided, so you can focus on enjoying the experience.

Whether you're young or old, our beginner sessions are suitable for everyone interested in learning the art of archery. No prior experience is required, and all skill levels are welcome.

Ready to give it a shot? Sign up for one of our upcoming beginner sessions and discover the excitement of archery!
